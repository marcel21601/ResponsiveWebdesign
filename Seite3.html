THE JOURNEY KNOW MORE Q&A

How does RideBuddy work?

You can register a ride or join an existing one in the app. Simply enter your starting point and destination to find or offer a carpool.

Is RideBuddy free to use?

Yes, RideBuddy is completely free for all users.

Who can use RideBuddy?

RideBuddy is designed specifically for students. You need a valid student ID to register and start using the app.

How does RideBuddy help reduce CO2 emissions?

By carpooling, fewer cars are on the road, which significantly reduces carbon dioxide emissions and helps combat climate change.

Is it safe to carpool with RideBuddy?

Yes, RideBuddy is designed with safety in mind. Only registered students can use the app, and you can review and connect with verified users before joining a ride.

What if my plans change after registering a ride?

You can easily update or cancel your ride in the app. Just make sure to notify the other participants as early as possible.
