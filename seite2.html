About RideBuddy

RideBuddy is an innovative app designed to combat climate change while making your daily commute more efficient and social.
As a student, you can use RideBuddy to register and share rides with fellow students heading to the same destination.

With our easy-to-use interface, you can:

  • Quickly register a ride or join an existing one.
  • Connect with fellow students who share your route and schedule.
  • Reduce your carbon footprint by carpooling to university.

By driving together, you contribute to a greener future and help to save CO2. RideBuddy is more than just an app; it is a movement to promote sustainability and build a community of environmentally conscious students.
